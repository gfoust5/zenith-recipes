✦ Built with Sam's Club ingredients · Member's Mark 93/7 beef swap
What You Need
  • 84g Member's Mark 93/7 lean ground beef Sam's Club
  • 5g (~2 tsp) low sodium taco seasoning
  • 1 Mission Carb Balance 8" flour tortilla Sam's Club
  • 1 corn tortilla
  • 34g (2 tbsp) Tostitos salsa con queso
  • 15g (~1/4 cup) shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 35g tomato, diced — optional
  • 28g (~2 tbsp) plain non-fat Greek yogurt Sam's Club
  • Cooking spray
What You Need To Do
  1. 01

    Cook 84g of 93/7 lean ground beef with 5g of low sodium taco seasoning in a pan over medium/high heat until browned.

  2. 02

    Heat 1 corn tortilla on a paper towel in the microwave for 60–80 seconds.

  3. 03

    Dice tomato if using.

  4. 04

    Lay the Mission Carb Balance tortilla flat. Layer cooked beef, salsa con queso, shredded lettuce, tomato, and Greek yogurt. Top with the crispy corn tortilla and fold into a crunchwrap shape.

  5. 05

    Spray a pan with cooking spray over medium/low heat. Place fold-side down to toast. Once golden, spray the top and flip. Remove when both sides are done to your liking.

Coach's Note

Swapped to 93/7 beef from Sam's — slightly more fat than 96/4 but still lean, comes in a bulk pack, and saves you money. The Mission Carb Balance tortilla from Sam's is the real workhorse here: 4g protein and only 15g carbs. Greek yogurt in place of sour cream keeps fat low and bumps protein. 329 calories for a full crunchwrap is still a major win.
