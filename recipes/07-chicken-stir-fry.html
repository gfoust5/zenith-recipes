High Protein Chicken Stir Fry

15 minutes, one pan, 50 grams of protein. This is the recipe you make when you're hungry now and don't have time to be patient about it.

430Calories
50gProtein
36gCarbs
9gFat
Prep 10 min Cook 15 min Servings 4 Macros shown per serving

Ingredients

  • 800gboneless skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 3 cupsbroccoli florets
  • 1 cupsnap peas
  • 1 cupshredded carrots
  • 3 tbsplow-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsprice vinegar
  • 1 tbspsesame oil
  • 2 tspcornstarch
  • 3 clovesgarlic, minced
  • 1 tbspfresh ginger, grated
  • 2 cupswhite rice, cooked

Directions

  1. Whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and cornstarch in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add chicken and cook 5–6 minutes until golden and cooked through. Remove to a plate.
  3. In the same pan, add broccoli, snap peas, and carrots. Stir fry 3–4 minutes over high heat.
  4. Add garlic and ginger. Cook 60 seconds.
  5. Return chicken to pan. Pour sauce over everything. Toss and cook 1–2 minutes until sauce thickens.
  6. Serve over white rice.

Coach's Note

High heat is the key — don't crowd the pan or it steams instead of stirs. If your stove runs weak, cook the chicken in two batches. The cornstarch in the sauce gives it that takeout-style glaze.
