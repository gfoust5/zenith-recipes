✦ Sam's Club 93/7 beef · Light mayo + sugar-free ketchup swap · Training day meal
What You Need
  • 168g Member's Mark 93/7 lean ground beef Sam's Club
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 brioche bun
  • 3 slices fully cooked bacon
  • 2 slices Velveeta cheese
  • 8g Hellmann's light mayo Swap
  • 8g sugar free ketchup Swap
What You Need To Do
  1. 01

    Split 168g of 93/7 ground beef into two equal balls. Season with salt and pepper. Smash flat in a screaming hot pan over medium-high heat. Cook until browned, flip once, cook through.

  2. 02

    Remove patties. Toast the brioche bun in the same pan until golden.

  3. 03

    Microwave 3 slices of bacon on a paper towel for 15–17 seconds.

  4. 04

    Assemble: bottom bun, first patty, 1 slice Velveeta, 1.5 slices bacon, second patty, second slice Velveeta, remaining bacon. Spread light mayo and sugar free ketchup on the top bun. Close and eat.

Coach's Note

54g of protein in a double smash burger. The 93/7 beef from Sam's is the workhorse — bulk pack, leaner than standard 80/20, and it still smashes beautifully. At 660 calories this earns its spot on a training day. Slot it in post-workout and let the protein do its job. Light mayo and sugar-free ketchup are saving you roughly 80 calories over the originals with zero sacrifice in taste.
