✦ Mission Carb Balance tortilla · Egg Beaters swap · Almost entirely from Sam's Club
What You Need
  • 6 Mission Carb Balance 8" tortillas Sam's Club
  • 230g Egg Beaters or Member's Mark liquid egg whites Swap
  • 98g shredded cheddar cheese
  • 18 pieces frozen potato crowns or hash brown bites
  • 224g Real Good lightly breaded chicken nuggets Protein Swap
  • Cooking spray
  • Salsa for serving — optional
What You Need To Do
  1. 01

    Air fry 224g of Real Good chicken nuggets at 330°F for 20 minutes.

  2. 02

    Air fry 18 potato crowns at 400°F for 10–12 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through.

  3. 03

    Spray a pan with cooking spray and scramble 230g of Egg Beaters over medium heat until just set — don't overcook.

  4. 04

    Divide eggs, 3 potato crowns, and nuggets evenly across all 6 tortillas. Top each with shredded cheddar.

  5. 05

    Fold and roll each burrito tight. To serve now, brown fold-side down in a lightly sprayed pan. To meal prep, wrap in foil and refrigerate up to 5 days.

Coach's Note

245 calories for a full breakfast burrito with 20g protein — that's a serious morning win. Make all six on Sunday and you've solved breakfast for the entire week without thinking about it once. The Carb Balance tortilla does the heavy lifting on keeping carbs in check at only 15g net. If you want to tighten the macros further, fat-free cheddar saves about 38 calories and 4g fat per burrito.
